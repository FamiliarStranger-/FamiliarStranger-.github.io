About LoL Jukebox


LoL Jukebox is an application that lets you enjoy your League of Legends matches with accompanying music! By using the Riot API to automatically detect when you join a match and check which champion you are currently playing (as well as what other champions are in the match), we create an appropriate playlist for your battle. Feel free to add any songs you feel may match, so others can also enjoy a fuller selection of songs!
